Community Rules

To keep EverythingTT a safe and professional environment, please follow these guidelines.

1 Content Standards

We strictly prohibit any content that contains offensive language, hate speech, or inappropriate terms. This applies to all user-generated content across the platform.

Enforcement: Usernames, Bios, Status Messages, Articles, and Comments are automatically scanned for flagged words.

2 User Identity

Usernames must be professional and free of any flagged terms. Impersonation of staff or other users is strictly prohibited.

3 Consequence of Violations

Users who violate these rules will be labeled as Rule-Breaker. Rule-Breakers are subject to administrative actions, including IP bans and content deletion.

4 Alt Account Policy

To prevent spam and maintain platform integrity, each user is limited to a maximum of 3 alt accounts per IP address. Accounts exceeding this limit will be automatically removed.

Exception: Verified administrators are exempt from this limit for testing and management purposes.

5 Automated Systems & AI

The use of automated scripts, scrapers, or AI-driven agents to interact with our backend, bypass security measures, or scrape user data is strictly prohibited. This is a violation of our Intellectual Property and User Privacy rights.

Legal Notice: Unauthorized access to private data storage is considered a cyber-security violation. We reserve the right to pursue legal action and provide IP data to relevant authorities.
